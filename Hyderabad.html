Explore Hyderabad

Discover the best tourist spots in Hyderabad!

1. Charminar

The Charminar is a historic mosque with four grand arches, built in 1591 by Sultan Muhammad Quli Qutb Shah. It's one of the most iconic monuments of Hyderabad.

Learn more about Charminar

2. Hussain Sagar Lake

Hussain Sagar is an artificial lake, famous for its large Buddha statue located in the middle of the lake. It's a popular spot for boating and scenic views.

Learn more about Hussain Sagar

3. Birla Mandir

Birla Mandir is a Hindu temple dedicated to Lord Venkateshwara, located on a hilltop offering panoramic views of the city.

Learn more about Birla Mandir

4. Golconda Fort

The Golconda Fort is a historic fort that served as the capital of medieval Golconda Sultanate. It's known for its advanced acoustics, architecture, and history.

Learn more about Golconda Fort

5. Nehru Zoological Park

The Nehru Zoological Park is one of the largest zoos in India, home to a wide variety of animals and birds. It's a great spot for family outings and nature lovers.

Learn more about Nehru Zoological Park
